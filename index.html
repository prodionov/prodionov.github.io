12 hours guide

10 am, coffee at Portuguese

On arrival to the Mile End station turn left and walk around the corner, where you will find an excellent Portuguese cafe coffee espresso. There are no hipster in-sight and you can quietly enjoy a cup of strong coffee with a sweet desert or a deep fried breakfast.
Prices are low, servie is friendly and unobtrusive
The atmosphere is great and the place is often full of locals. Personally, I would recommend a rather standard doubel espresson with a Pastel de Nata
